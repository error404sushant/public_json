Campus Mesh is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and strictly prohibit any related content or behavior on our platform.

---

## Zero Tolerance Policy

Campus Mesh strictly prohibits:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Sexual exploitation or abuse of minors
- Grooming or any attempt to engage minors in sexual activity
- Any content that endangers the safety or wellbeing of children

Violations of this policy result in immediate account suspension and reporting to appropriate authorities.

---

## User Eligibility

Campus Mesh is designed for **college students and adult users**.  
The app is **not intended for children under the age of 13**.

---

## Content Moderation & Reporting

- Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app
- All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team
- Content that violates child safety policies is removed immediately

---

## Law Enforcement Cooperation

Campus Mesh complies with all applicable child safety laws and cooperates with regional and national law enforcement authorities when legally required.

---

## Compliance With Laws

We follow applicable child protection regulations, including laws related to the prevention, detection, and reporting of child sexual abuse material.
